About the Festival

More than books — a movement of ideas.

Kollam Literature Fest 2026 is a premier cultural and intellectual gathering designed to celebrate the power of literature, storytelling, and ideas. Set in the vibrant cultural landscape of Kerala, it brings together writers, thinkers, artists and audiences from across the country.

This festival is not just about books — it is about conversations that inspire change, narratives that challenge perspectives, and voices that redefine the future. From engaging panel discussions to immersive cultural experiences, the event creates a space where creativity meets intellect.

  • Star conversations
  • Live music & band nights
  • Panel discussions
  • Workshops & talks
  • Book stalls & exhibitions
  • Art installations & photo spots
