About the Festival

More than books — a movement of ideas.

Kollam Literature Fest 2026 is a premier cultural and intellectual gathering designed to celebrate the power of literature, storytelling, and ideas. Set in the vibrant cultural landscape of Kerala, it brings together writers, thinkers, artists and audiences from across the country.

This festival is not just about books — it is about conversations that inspire change, narratives that challenge perspectives, and voices that redefine the future. From engaging panel discussions to immersive cultural experiences, the event creates a space where creativity meets intellect.

  • Star conversations
  • Live music & band nights
  • Panel discussions
  • Workshops & talks
  • Book stalls & exhibitions
  • Art installations & photo spots

A Cultural Movement

More than a festival — a quiet revolution

When thousands of readers, students, artists and changemakers gather in one place, something shifts. Stories become catalysts, conversations become commitments, and a town becomes a stage for the future.

📣

Voices that rise

From spoken-word poets to veteran novelists, the fest amplifies youth, regional and underrepresented voices — turning literature and music into living social commentary.

🤝

Dialogue over division

Panels and open conversations bring generations, languages and ideologies to the same table — building empathy and challenging perspectives.

🌱

Youth at the centre

Students and young creators don't just attend — they perform, publish and lead, discovering pathways into writing, media and the arts.

🪔

A regional renaissance

The festival puts Kollam and Kerala on the national cultural map, celebrating local identity while inviting the whole country in.

The Outcome

What this movement leaves behind

✦ New creators

Emerging writers, poets and performers discovered and given a real platform and audience.

✦ Stronger civic dialogue

A culture of listening and debate that keeps rippling outward long after the festival ends.

✦ Pride & local economy

Renewed pride in Kerala's literary heritage and a boost to local culture, tourism and trade.

✦ A lasting platform

A scalable annual movement, designed to grow and deepen its impact edition after edition.
