The Vision

From exhibit to experience.

Exhibitions tell stories. VR lets you live them. This project transforms the extraordinary life of Sri Sri Ravi Shankar — meditator, peacemaker, humanitarian — into a first-person journey visitors step inside, choose within, and carry with them long after the headset comes off.

Immersive

Be inside the story. Stand at the Himalayan dawn. Witness reconciliation in Kashmir. No screen can do this.

Interactive

Eight decision points let each visitor chart a personal path. No two journeys through this life are exactly the same.

Inclusive

Hindi and English narration. Accessible design. Distributable globally via SideQuest — no gatekeepers, no barriers.

The Journey

Five acts. One lifetime. Your choices.

A branching cinematic narrative across 45–60 minutes of immersive experience.

I

Act One

90 seconds

Beginnings

The Himalayas at dawn. A child sits in stillness while the world wakes. You are present inside the formative years of a life that would become a movement.

Your choice: Follow the breath of early morning meditation, or walk with the young student through sacred texts.
II

Act Two

3 minutes

Vision Emerges

1981. A vision takes form. The Art of Living Foundation is born from a single question: can ancient wisdom heal the modern mind?

Two paths: The Meditation Path — witness the birth of Sudarshan Kriya — or the Teaching Path, following the first courses across the world.
III

Act Three

2 minutes

The Peacemaker

Kashmir. Ayodhya. Two of the most charged places on earth. You stand at the centre of dialogues that brought adversaries into the same room — and changed history quietly.

Three perspectives: The Mediator, the Community Leader, or the Journalist witnessing reconciliation unfold.
IV

Act Four

3 minutes

Scale of Compassion

A movement at planetary scale. Farmers, forests, and futures transformed across 182 countries. Choose where you stand in the story of service.

Three missions: Mission Green Earth (100M+ trees), Women Empowerment, or Skill Development for 475,000+ youth.
V

Act Five

2 minutes

Legacy

One billion lives. A vision that reaches forward. The final act is personal — a moment of quiet reflection where the journey becomes yours to continue.

The question: What will you carry forward? A personal reflection prompt closes the experience.
What Makes It Special

Built for the moment. Ready for the world.

Every technical decision serves the story.

Cinematic AI Visuals

AI-generated imagery rendered for 360° VR — photorealistic environments placing visitors inside real historical moments.

Spatial 3D Audio

Sound that moves with you. Himalayan winds, crowd resonance, intimate narration — all positionally rendered.

8+ Choice Points

A branching narrative that makes each visit unique. No two visitors take exactly the same path through the story.

Bilingual Narration

Full narration in Hindi and English. A single toggle switches the entire experience — no restart required.

Native Meta Quest 3

Optimised for Quest 3's mixed-reality capabilities and controller-free hand-tracking interactions.

Globally Distributable

Publish via SideQuest from day one. No App Lab approval delays. Reach audiences everywhere, immediately.

Built in Quill 2.0

No-code VR production means faster iteration, lower costs, and easy content updates without a rebuild.

Scalable Platform

Architecture designed to port to Apple Vision Pro and PlayStation VR as the platform market matures.

Production

Defined deliverables at every stage. No ambiguity about what happens when.
