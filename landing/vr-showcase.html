Gurudev Sri Sri Ravishankar

A Teacher.
A Humanitarian.
A Beacon of Peace.

For over four decades, Gurudev Sri Sri Ravishankar has walked the world teaching one simple truth — that joy, peace, and love are your nature. Not something to search for, but something to uncover.

Founder of the Art of Living Foundation, his programs have touched hundreds of millions across every continent, every faith, every language.

This VR experience brings you into his world — his journey, his guidance, his presence — so you may discover a little of that peace for yourself.

Four Doorways

What Awaits You Inside

Each world is crafted for wonder, stillness, and transformation. Put on the headset. Let the outside fall away.

01 — Life Journey

Gurudev's Life Journey

From a childhood in Tamil Nadu to the Himalayan heights, from intimate gatherings to arenas of thousands — walk the path of an extraordinary life through a branching, cinematic story only you can navigate.

02 — Meditation

Immersive Guided Meditation

In virtual reality there are no distractions. Only the breath, the stillness, and Gurudev's voice. Be guided through Sudarshan Kriya into a deep, enveloping silence you may never have touched before.

03 — Ashram

Sacred Ashram Tours

Wander the verdant, flower-scented pathways of the Art of Living International Centre. Step inside meditation halls, join morning prayers, and feel the gentle energy of a place where thousands come to rest.

04 — Satsang

Live Satsangs with Gurudev

Be in the front row. The music rises, laughter fills the air, and then — a silence deeper than you expected. Join a satsang with Gurudev in full 360° immersion, surrounded by thousands of joyful souls.
