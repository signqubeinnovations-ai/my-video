Common Questions

Everything you need
to know

Do I need prior meditation experience?
Yes — these are advanced programs. You should have completed at least one Art of Living course (Happiness Program / Part 1 or equivalent). This ensures the retreat can go deep without teaching basics.
What does "silence" actually mean here?
During most of the retreat, participants maintain noble silence — no speaking, no phones, no reading, no writing. This isn't a restriction; it's a gift. When you stop talking, a deeper conversation with yourself becomes possible. Facilitators are available for essential communication.
What is the cost?
We follow a contribution-based model with a recommended amount covering accommodation, sattvic meals, and logistics. No one is turned away for financial reasons — if cost is a concern, please mention it during our call. All amounts are fully transparent.
Are these affiliated with the Art of Living Foundation?
We are a group of Art of Living volunteers who organise these programs independently with the spirit of seva. The programs draw from Art of Living teachings and practices. Please confirm current affiliation status with us directly.
What should I bring?
Comfortable, loose, natural-fibre clothing (white or light colours preferred), a yoga mat if you have one, personal medications, and an open heart. We'll send a full checklist after you register. Leave your laptop at home.
Can I join if I have health conditions?
Please mention any physical or mental health conditions when you register. Our team will review and advise. Some conditions may require a doctor's clearance; we'll guide you through this with care and confidentiality.
