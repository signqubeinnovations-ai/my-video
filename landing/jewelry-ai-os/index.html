FAQ

Good questions, clear answers

Will the AI sound like my brand?

Yes. AuraOS is trained on your products, pricing, tone and policies, so every reply feels like it comes from your best salesperson — in the customer's preferred language.

Do I need to replace my current systems?

No. AuraOS layers on top of WhatsApp, Instagram, your website, billing and inventory. You keep your tools — we make them work together intelligently.

How fast can we go live?

Most brands are live within days. We import your catalogue, connect your channels and train the AI, then go live with you supervising before it runs autonomously.

Is my data and my customers' data safe?

Absolutely. Your data is encrypted, isolated to your account, never sold, and handled in line with privacy best practices and applicable regulations.

What if a customer wants a human?

The AI seamlessly hands off to your team with full context whenever needed — and your staff can jump into any conversation at any time.
